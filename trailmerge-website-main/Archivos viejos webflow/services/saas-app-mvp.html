Our Process

Learn from users

We use a toolkit of UX research methodologies to guide and inform our user-centered design work.

Build a design system

Based on your design goals and UX best practices, we create reusable UI standards and components in Figma.

Define UX architecture

We map out data and user flows so we can design comprehensive, easy-to-use UIs and interactions.

Design and test mockups

Using increasing levels of fidelity, we craft mockups and prototypes that can be tested and iterated on.

Prepare designs for development

We hand over dev-friendly mockups that are inspectable, annotated, and accompanied by detailed user stories.

Provide ongoing Agile support

We work within your team's Agile ecosystem, supporting your product managers and devs in their efforts.
