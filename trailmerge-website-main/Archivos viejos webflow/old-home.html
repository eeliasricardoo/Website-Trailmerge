Our Founder

Founder Mark Tegtmeier brings years of design experience to Trailmerge. With a background in cultural/linguistic studies and branding, he has worked with early stage startups, design and software agencies, government, and enterprise, driving them further in their product vision.

During his time in the field, Mark has served as a mentor at the US Air Force Academy, taught junior designers at General Assembly, and advised The Home Depot on design for multilingual users. He has spoken at events such as TEDx, World Information Architecture Day, and NewTech Denver.

A husband of one, father of four and urban homesteader, Mark loves developing tech talent and coming alongside founders with ambitious visions for their products and companies.
